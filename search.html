Search bar searches as you type. There might be a delay depending on the results included!

To search exact match use `+`
For example: `+aqara +door ` will show only devices with "Aqara" and "Door" in their name.

You can exclude any term from search results with `-`.
For example: `aqara -door ` will show all devices named "Aqara" that don't have "Door" in ther name.
